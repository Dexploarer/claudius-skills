# Community Challenges - Grow Through Doing

**Engaging weekly and monthly challenges to level up your skills and community status**

---

## How Challenges Work

### Weekly Challenges (Every Monday)
- New challenge posted in community
- 7 days to complete
- Share your submission
- Get feedback from community
- Winners announced Friday

### Monthly Mega-Challenges (First of Month)
- Larger, more ambitious projects
- 30 days to complete
- Multiple categories/tracks
- Prizes and recognition
- Showcase event at end

### Ongoing Challenges (Anytime)
- Evergreen challenges
- Complete at your own pace
- No deadline
- Still get recognition

## Tips for Success

### Winning Strategies

**1. Start Simple**
- Better to finish something small than abandon something big
- Add polish over features
- Playable > Feature-complete

**2. Use AI Effectively**
- Ask specific questions
- Iterate on responses
- Document what works
- Share your prompts

**3. Get Feedback Early**
- Share WIP screenshots
- Ask for opinions
- Iterate based on feedback
- Test with others

**4. Polish Matters**
- Juice your game (effects, sounds)
- Clean UI
- Clear instructions
- Handle edge cases

**5. Document Journey**
- Take screenshots during development
- Note what you learn
- Share struggles and wins
- Others learn from you

## FAQs

**Q: Can I submit to multiple challenges?**
A: Yes! Complete as many as you want.

**Q: What if I don't finish in time?**
A: Submit what you have! Incomplete entries still get feedback and participation points.

**Q: Can I use old games?**
A: No, must be built during challenge period.

**Q: Can I work in a team?**
A: Unless challenge specifies solo, teams are welcome!

**Q: What if I'm a beginner?**
A: Perfect! Challenges have different difficulty levels. Start with speed challenges.

**Q: Can I use paid assets?**
A: Yes, as long as you have license. Credit them.

**Q: Can I win multiple prizes?**
A: Yes! Win every challenge if you can!

**Q: How are ties broken?**
A: Community votes, then earliest submission.
