# Strategic Planning Assistant

**Trigger phrases:**
- "create a strategic plan"
- "develop strategy for"
- "strategic planning for"
- "help me strategize"
- "build a strategy"

**Description:**
Facilitates comprehensive strategic planning using proven frameworks. Helps organizations and individuals develop clear strategies with actionable roadmaps for achieving long-term objectives.

## What this skill does:

When you need to develop a strategy, this skill will:

1. **Analyze the current state:**
   - SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)
   - Market/competitive landscape
   - Resource assessment
   - Constraints and challenges

2. **Define the future state:**
   - Vision and mission alignment
   - Strategic objectives
   - Success metrics
   - Time horizons

3. **Develop the strategy:**
   - Strategic pillars/themes
   - Key initiatives
   - Resource allocation
   - Prioritization

4. **Create implementation roadmap:**
   - Phased approach
   - Quick wins vs. long-term bets
   - Milestones and metrics
   - Accountability structure

5. **Risk and contingency planning:**
   - Potential obstacles
   - Mitigation strategies
   - Alternative scenarios

## Frameworks applied:

### SWOT Analysis
- **Strengths** - Internal advantages
- **Weaknesses** - Internal limitations
- **Opportunities** - External favorable conditions
- **Threats** - External challenges

### OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
- **Objectives** - What we want to achieve (qualitative)
- **Key Results** - How we measure success (quantitative)

### Porter's Five Forces
- Competitive rivalry
- Supplier power
- Buyer power
- Threat of substitution
- Threat of new entry

### Ansoff Matrix (Growth strategies)
- Market Penetration (existing product, existing market)
- Market Development (existing product, new market)
- Product Development (new product, existing market)
- Diversification (new product, new market)

### Blue Ocean Strategy
- Eliminate - What industry factors should be eliminated?
- Reduce - What should be reduced well below standard?
- Raise - What should be raised well above standard?
- Create - What should be created that industry has never offered?
